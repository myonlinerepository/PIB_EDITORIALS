Key Objective ?  With the aim to reduce the expenditure incurred by patients on treatment of cancer and heart diseases, the Ministry for Health & Family Welfare, has launched the Affordable Medicines and Reliable Implants for Treatment (AMRIT) programme [Though initially started for cancer and cardio diseases, Other diseases related medicines are also covered] [Drugs and other medical tools like stents are sold at highly discounted rates]

Nodal Agency ? HLL Lifecare Limited, a Government of India enterprise [control of Ministry of Health and Family Welfare] was made nodal agency to implement Amrit pharmacies across the country.
