Objectives of EMRS
--------------------
The objective of EMRS is to provide quality middle and high level education to
Scheduled Tribe (ST) students in remote areas, not only to enable them to avail of reservation
in high and professional educational courses and as jobs in government and public and
private sectors but also to have access to the best opportunities in education at par with the non ST population

Constitutional provisions: under Article 275(1) of the Constitution of India
