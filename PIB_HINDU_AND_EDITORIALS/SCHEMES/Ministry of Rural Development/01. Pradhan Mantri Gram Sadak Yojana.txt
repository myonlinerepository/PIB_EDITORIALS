ENGLISH: Prime Minister's Rural Roads Scheme

1. The Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY), was launched by the Govt. of India to provide connectivity to unconnected Habitations as part of a poverty reduction strategy
2. As part of the PMGSY programme, about 1.67 lakh Unconnected Habitations are eligible for coverage under the programme
3. The Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana(PMGSY) is a 100% Centrally Sponsored Scheme
4. Initiative by Ministry of Rural Development
5. Also this programme connects the areas with difficult terrain and LWE affected zones.
OBJECTIVES ? 
The primary objective of the PMGSY is to provide Connectivity, by way of an All-weather Road to the eligible unconnected Habitations in the rural areas ( with a population of 250 persons and above.)
